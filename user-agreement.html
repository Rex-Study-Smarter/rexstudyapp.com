1. About Rex

Rex is an AI-powered iOS study platform designed to help students learn more effectively. Rex provides:

  • AI Tutor: a conversational large language model (LLM) tutor that answers questions, explains concepts, and engages in educational dialogue.
  • Study Boards: interactive digital canvases where you can organize notes, charts, whiteboards, and study widgets.
  • Whiteboard Tools: free-form drawing and annotation tools for visual learning.
  • Chart Widgets: interactive subject-specific visual learning widgets (e.g., math graphs, science diagrams).
  • Voice Features: voice dictation for input and AI text-to-speech for responses, enabling a hands-free study experience.
  • Note-taking Tools: digital notepads integrated with AI assistance.

Rex is an educational tool and is not a substitute for professional instruction, tutoring services, or academic counseling.
